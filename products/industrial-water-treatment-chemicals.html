Industrial Water Treatment Chemicals – SANYOL® WT

According to a recent report of Government & Independent organisations, about 22% of fresh water is being used by Industries. In India, comparing per person availability of water in 2001 at 1816 Litres to 1367 Litres in 2031 is being projected which is a matter of concern. With rising population and rising demand of industries the situation might get further deteriorated for availability of water. We need to act and need to act fast. Our target should be to maximize recyclability and reusability of this 22% of Water as it will also reduce the fresh water contaminations caused due to mixing of Industrial streams and Fresh Water stream.

To solve this global issue, we need to develop solutions and methods for better utilisation of resources in an Industry for water & Effluent Water Treatment. With high end Waste Water Recycling technologies and government initiatives of treating city water supply and Sewage waste water, it has now become a need of an hour to support these activities by the best Water treatment Chemicals which are free of toxins or any hazard to not be added in any upstream water. We at San Polychem with a strong focus on Research & Development, have developed solutions which will not only solve the Industrial & Municipal Water Problems but will also keep a check on protecting the environment and living organisms for better future.

San Polychem, along with highly experienced Industry Experts has developed a range of products which are suitable to solve complex water conditions in any given geographical conditions. We are proud to launch some Industry-First products which has not only change the market dynamics but are also helping in environmental issues caused by plastic waste generation of packing materials.

SANYOL® WT range of speciality products cater to various Industrial Water Treatment needs in Boilers, Cooling Towers, RO Modules, UF Modules, DM & Mixed Bed Units across sectors like Power, Cement, Textiles, Automotives, Chemical Plants, Oil & Gas, Distilleries & Municipal Water Treatment. The product range is developed based on various critical and practical scenarios backed by rigorous testing at our highly equipped Lab, which will give desired results at an optimum dosing levels, thereby controlling costs. The SANYOL® WT range includes Antiscalants, Oxygen Scavenges, Dispersants, Corrosion Inhibitors, pH Boosters, Biocides, Cleaning or Descaling Chemicals. We also serve to any special requirements for typical water conditions based on customers’ requirement in our brand SANYOL® FLEX.
