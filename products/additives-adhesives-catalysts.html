Additives, Adhesives & Catalysts – SANYOL® AD

San Polychem always have a holistic approach towards solving Industrial Problems through Chemicals. We are always looked forward from our clients for giving them a best and complete end to end solution for solving their needs. We have developed a Product range SANYOL® AD to cater to small yet useful requirements from clients helping them in process excellence in their plants.

SANYOL® AD also has a range for Polyurethane-Adhesive based flexible packaging industry where we cater the clients with raw materials for flexible packaging. Flexible packaging is widely used in food items due to its many advantages like light weight, easy to fill, long lasting printing, sealing etc. Flexible packaging is superior to any other packaging like plastics, metal, paper or glass in the present times. It is either a solvent based or solvent free solution based on the application. It is based on a two component system which works on the principal of Polyol & Isoprepolymer being coated together to form a Polyurethane layer which gives good adhesion properties for strong bonding of multiple layers in a roll.
